CIRCUIT BOX MECHANICS:
 <ConnectionPanel> -> <input/output> nodes -> <link> contains outside wire IDs connected to the pins of other component/circuitbox/reactor/etc
 <ItemContainer contained="1,2,3,4,,,,"> with commas contains components IDs inside circuitbox
 <ItemContainer contained="1;2;3;4;;;;"> with semicolons contains wires IDs inside circuitbox
